About Letters 2 God

Letters 2 God is a global prayer chain designed to connect believers through shared prayer, reflection, and encouragement.

The app allows users to read prayer requests from around the world, pray for others, and acknowledge those prayers in a meaningful, respectful way.

How It Works

Prayer requests are displayed from newest to oldest, allowing easy browsing. Each request includes a short preview, and you may open the full request to read more details.

When you pray for a request, you may mark it as Prayer Sent. This records your prayer and returns you to the main list.

Each request includes a prayer counter so the requester can see how many people have prayed for them.

Prayer Categories

Each prayer request is submitted under one of the following categories:

  • Blessing
  • Fellowship
  • Financial
  • Health
  • Intercession
  • Spiritual
  • Supplication
  • Worship
  • Other

Commitment to Prayer

Every prayer request is acknowledged. Each submission will receive at least one Prayer Sent response so no request is ever left unseen.

Submitting Requests

Submitting a prayer request costs $0.99. This small fee helps maintain the platform and encourages thoughtful, sincere participation.

App Maintenance

At the beginning of each month, older prayer requests are archived. Archived requests remain accessible depending on your subscription status.
